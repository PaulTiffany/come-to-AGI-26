Extended layman’s abstract

What is the paper actually saying?

A research system should reveal the state of an inquiry, not merely manufacture its cleanest-sounding answer. The Hypothesis Surface is a proposed workspace for keeping evidence, uncertainty, conflict, absence, and next actions visible at the same time.

The problem

Most AI systems are built to produce an answer. A research system has a harder job: it must track where each claim came from, how confident it should be, which claims conflict, what remains unknown, and what observation could change the picture. Compress those distinctions into one smooth paragraph and the system may sound intelligent while concealing the real state of the inquiry.

What the Surface keeps separate
01 · Evidence

Claims carry fibers

A claim is not allowed to appear alone. Its fiber records confidence, conditions, resolution, and supporting witnesses. Similar sentences can therefore remain different epistemic objects.

02 · Conflict

Disagreement leaves a certificate

A conflict is not averaged away or handed to another model for a verdict. It becomes a durable, typed record of what failed to fit and what kind of revision might matter.

03 · Absence

Unknown regions become voids

A void records what is missing, which claims border the gap, and what evidence could resolve it. Absence becomes structured research state rather than an invitation to guess.

04 · Imagination

Voids generate hypotheses

The system may extend a boundary, expose a hidden comparison axis, transport an analogy, or invert an obstruction. Every proposal enters at low confidence with its origin attached.

One measured mechanism
0.36 → 0.00lexical masking rate

Anti-masking limits how certain the answer may sound.

The final wording may hedge, qualify, or narrow a claim, but it may not express more confidence than the attached evidence supports. In the paper’s small deterministic evaluation, masking measured about 0.36 with the rule disabled and 0.00 with it enabled. That demonstrates a mechanism-level behavior change—not perfect honesty, full semantic understanding, or universal performance.

How the proposal is tested and extended
Inspectable implementation

A deliberately plain pipeline

The proof of concept uses CPU-only Python, inspectable rules, and no language-model judge deciding which claims are true. It extracts claims, attaches fibers, propagates confidence, scores conflict, maps voids, proposes hypotheses, and checks rendered language. The claim is not that lexical machinery solves science; it is that epistemic bookkeeping can be explicit and falsifiable before richer semantics are added.

Mathematical companions

Differentiation without erasure

The paper connects claim differentiation and integration to a broader geometric picture. Public companions develop carefully scoped versions of the Fuzzy Fundamental Theorem, Observer-Relative Stokes, a masking-to-residue bridge, and observer-bounded critical scaling. Their assumptions and limits remain part of the claims; they do not turn every metaphor into a theorem.

The governing boundary

Not “let an AI do science alone.”

The central proposal is almost the opposite. A bounded research agent may explore, compare, and propose while remaining unable to silently turn its own output into authoritative knowledge. Provenance, uncertainty, conflict, negative results, and open questions remain visible to the humans who decide what counts as evidence and what happens next. The Surface tries to make that discipline structural rather than aspirational.
